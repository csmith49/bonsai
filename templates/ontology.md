## Journal

Daily notes, usually organized into blocks separated by horizontal rules (`---`).

## Maps

Curated views into the rest of the knowledge base.

## People

Notes on people who recur across the knowledge base.

## Refs

References to primary sources. Each reference should keep source metadata in frontmatter, especially `kind` and `url` when available.

## Scripts

Command-line utilities for navigating and maintaining the vault.

## ZK

Short for [[zettelkasten]], a method for developing atomic, linked notes.
